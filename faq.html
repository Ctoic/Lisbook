Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Lisbook?

Lisbook is an audiobook and music streaming platform that helps users access a variety of books and playlists from different genres and authors.

How can I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the 'Sign Up' button on the home page, and filling in your email and password details.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, Lisbook has mobile apps for both Android and iOS that allow you to access your favorite audiobooks on the go.

Can I download audiobooks for offline listening?

Yes, Lisbook allows premium users to download audiobooks for offline listening.

Is there a free version of Lisbook?

Yes, Lisbook offers a free version with limited access to books and music, supported by ads.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime by navigating to the 'Account Settings' and selecting the 'Cancel Subscription' option.

Does Lisbook have a family plan?

Yes, Lisbook offers a family plan that allows up to 5 members to share one subscription at a discounted price.

Can I create my own playlists?

Yes, Lisbook allows users to create and share personalized playlists of their favorite audiobooks and music.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on 'Forgot Password' on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email.

What formats are supported for audiobooks?

Lisbook supports a variety of audio formats, including MP3, AAC, and WAV for audiobooks.

How can I share audiobooks with friends?

You can share audiobooks with friends via social media or by sending them a direct link from within the app.

Can I listen to audiobooks while offline?

Yes, once downloaded, you can listen to your audiobooks offline without needing an internet connection.

Does Lisbook offer audiobooks in different languages?

Yes, Lisbook offers audiobooks in a variety of languages including English, Spanish, French, and more.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach customer support by going to the 'Help' section in the app or by emailing us at support@lisbook.com.
